规则/资料中心 Rules/Resources Hub

RoboMaster 高校赛务

欢迎来到RoboMaster 规则/资料中心！此站点由RoboMaster组委会建设与维护，集中提供参赛所需的官方资料，旨在帮助队伍高效获取信息。您可以通过左侧导航或搜索功能，快速定位所需内容。希望这里能成为您备赛和学习的首选平台，助力每一支队伍实现突破与成长。

内容覆盖范围

  • 赛事规则：比赛规则手册、机器人制作规范手册、参赛手册、串口协议
  • 赛事产品：裁判系统（含机载端模块、赛事引擎）、机器人套件&配件、RM Assistant的产品详情、资料下载、购买&售后政策
  • 技术评审：技术评审细则、各环节须知
  • 品牌宣传：品牌宣传素材包
  • 招商指引：招商手册、招商指南
  • 培训体系：参赛攻略、课程沙龙、开源资料

Welcome to the RoboMaster Rules/Resource Center! The RoboMaster Organizing Committee builds and maintains this site to provide participants with streamlined access to all essential official resources. You can quickly locate the content you need through the left-hand navigation or the search function. We hope this platform will become your go-to resource for preparation and learning, empowering every team to achieve breakthroughs and growth.

Content Coverage

  • Rulebooks: rules manuals, robot building specifications manual, participant manuals, serial protocol
  • Event Products: referee system (including onboard modules and the RoboMaster Champion), robot kits & accessories, RM Assistant product details, downloads, purchase & after-sales policies
  • Technical Assessment: mid-term & final robot assessment criteria, instructions for each stage
  • Event Promotion Kit: promotional material package
  • Event Sponsorship Guide: sponsorship guidelines
  • Learning Labs: participation guide, course salon, open-source materials
